Jack's big movie list

A list of some films I like.

Dune (parts 1 and 2)

Well-paced, beautiful films with particularly good direction from Denis Villeneuve.

LotR trilogy

Absolute classics. Epic and immersive.

The Green Knight

An odd medieval fantasy film, adapted from the 14th-century poem "Sir Gawain and the Green Knight". Difficult to follow, but I was interested all the way through.
